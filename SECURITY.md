# Security Policy

## Security

⚠️ **IMPORTANT:**
- **NEVER** commit `.env` file to git!
- Keep cookies secure
- Do not share your cookies with others
- Regularly update cookies (they may expire)

The `.env` file is already added to `.gitignore` to protect your data.
